MY JOURNEY....

1997..

My journey started way back in 1997 when I joined the British Army Infantry in what is now known as the rifles .  At 17 I would soon get my first experience and develope a passion for traveling with a 2 month trip to Canada.
A passion that continues to this day...

..2003..

Operational tours and trips including the following; Northern Ireland, Gibralter, Kuwait, America, Belize, Jamaica, Malaysia, Kenya. Also during this period promotion course for Lance Corporal and Corporal, the latter being conducted at The Infantry Battle School.

..2004..

Kosovo, and it was around this time that I started to contemplate a career transition into the corporate security sector.

2006

Working in Afghanistan, predominately in the Helmand province and surrounding areas.

2008

Working in Iraq and operating in and around the vicinty of Baghdad. Countrywide trips would also be undertaken to Mosul, Kirkuk and surrounding areas, Al-Falluja, Ramadi and down south To Basra and the Kuwait border. Hard work but (mostly) great people and good memories.

2009

Continuing to work all over Iraq and experiencing extremely hot summers. In Basra one summer's day, the temperature reached 51 degrees which was slightly uncomfortable, especially whilst wearing all the protective clothing!

..2011..

One of my favourite trips was Machu Picchu, a truly majestical place where the Andes spiralled and pierced through the low lying clouds. I was also living in Finland by this time and getting to know the Nordic country. In 2013 I decided to go back to school, this time in Helsinki.

2016..

A long term ambition of mine was to complete the marathon des sables and in April 2016 I finally got the chance to participate! Carrying all the food and eqipment needed for the 6 day, 257km event through the Sahara desert truly was an experience to remember. By far the most challenging aspect was the preperation and training, as running in the Finnish winter of -25 was hardly ideal for the +40 Sahara! Number of fingers represent trips taken during the previous night to the toilet!!
I also finished my Cambridge AICE diploma from high school and decided to go to university and complete a Master's degree in England.

2019

It was around this time that I had my first experience in Web Development when I attended a coding bootCamp in Helsinki. Here I was introduced to front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, Javascript and React. For the first time in my life I was more worried about 'spacing' between elements on a web page rather than 'spacing' whilst on patrol in some hostile environment! :)
Following the birth of my Son, I took some time off which brings us to 2021 where I have just completed Codecademy's Front-end engineer course. It is certainly not easy, but I shall continue to learn, code, and create projects in the future to become a more valuable employee....
